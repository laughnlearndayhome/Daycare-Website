Programs Tailored for Growth

At Ln'L Family Day Home Agency, we offer a range of carefully crafted programs to support children from infancy through their early school years. Each program focuses on age-appropriate learning, social-emotional development, and creative exploration within a safe and loving environment.

Infant Program (0-18 months)

Our infant program provides a secure and nurturing environment for your little ones. We focus on sensory exploration, language development, and building strong attachments through gentle care and interactive play.

  • Low caregiver-to-child ratios
  • Individualized feeding and sleep schedules
  • Tummy time and gross motor skill development
Enroll Now

Toddler Program (18-36 months)

The toddler program encourages independence and curiosity. Through guided play, toddlers develop language skills, fine motor coordination, and social interaction in a stimulating setting.

  • Potty training support
  • Building vocabulary and communication
  • Creative arts and music activities
Enroll Now

Preschool Program (3-5 years)

Our preschool program prepares children for kindergarten by fostering pre-reading, math, and problem-solving skills. We balance structured learning with plenty of free play and outdoor exploration.

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Science and nature exploration
  • Group projects and collaborative play
Enroll Now

School-Age Program (5-12 years)

This program offers a supportive and engaging environment for school-aged children before and after school hours, and during school breaks. We provide homework support, recreational activities, and opportunities for social development.

  • Homework assistance
  • Organized games and outdoor play
  • Creative arts and STEM activities
