Enrollment Made Easy

Learn about our straightforward process to join our loving community.

How to Enroll Your Child

Inquire & Tour

Start by contacting us to schedule a visit. We'll show you around, discuss our programs, and answer any questions you have.

Application Submission

After your tour, complete our online enrollment application form. Please have your child's medical and emergency contact information ready.

Interview & Assessment

We'll arrange a meeting for you and your child to get to know our educators and ensure the program is the perfect fit.

Confirmation & Orientation

Upon acceptance, we'll confirm your child's placement and schedule an orientation session to finalize paperwork and prepare for their first day!

Required Documents & Information

  • Completed Enrollment Application Form
  • Child's Immunization Records
  • Emergency Contact Information (minimum 3 contacts)
  • Medical Information and Allergy Details
  • Photo ID of Parents/Guardians
  • Proof of Address

Please note: Additional documents may be required based on local regulations. Our team will guide you through the complete list during your inquiry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Friday, 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., excluding public holidays.

Do you offer part-time enrollment?

Yes, we offer various part-time schedules depending on program availability. Please inquire for details.

What is your sick child policy?

For the health and safety of all children and staff, we adhere to strict guidelines regarding sick children. Please refer to our detailed policy available upon request.
